HAMMER SMASHING

Smash Your Odds with Hammer Probability!

Hammer Probability is an innovative project that combines the excitement of hammer smashing with the principles of probability theory. By using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I have created a virtual hammer that allows you to smash through various objects and see the likelihood of different outcomes. which you can adjust and experiment with to gain a better understanding of how probability works. Hammer Probability offers a fun and engaging way to learn about probability theory through hands-on experimentation.
